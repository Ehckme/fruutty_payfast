3️⃣ Getting Started with Fruutty

1 • Account Creation & Login

To create an account and sign up with Fruutty, follow these steps:

  • Go to https://fruutty.com. Click the spinning Fruutty token coin.
  • Click the Signup button. Fill in:
    • Username (random or preferred)
    • Valid email address
    • Custom password (6–16 characters)
  • Click Signup.

2 • OTP Confirmation & Email Validation

  • After signup, you will be taken to the OTP Confirmation page.
  • Check your email inbox or spam for noreply@fruutty.com and the "CONFIRM" email.
  • Click the confirmation button or copy the OTP code into the input field.
  • After confirmation, you'll see "Account successfully confirmed". Click Go to Login.

3 • Login to Fruutty

  • Enter your registered email and password.
  • Click Login to access Fruutty.

4 • Inside Fruutty Application

The first page is the Scanner. Standard layout includes:

  • Buy Tokens: Top-left button. Click to view token bundles (10, 50, 100, 200, 300, 500, 1000, 2000, 3000, 5000). Clicking an amount takes you to PayFast for payment.
  • Hamburger Menu: Click to reveal navigation drawer:
    • Scan: Scan tokens for money or product purchases. Approve tokens and view receipt.
    • Token: Create money or product tokens. Enter amount and product name (if applicable), then click Create Token.
    • Send: Peer-to-peer token sending to valid Fruutty accounts. Enter recipient email and amount, then click Send.
    • Market: Community marketplace with Fruutty player. Play featured music and click ads to visit advertiser sites.

5 • Security Features

Fruutty uses:

  • Argon2 for secure password hashing
  • Google reCAPTCHA for login validation
  • JWT for encryption
  • Custom Uconra security validation encryption (available on PyPI)

6 • Support & Contact

For any queries, contact support@fruutty.com. Response time: 24 hours to 7 days.
