1. What Are Cookies? Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by a web server when you visit a website or use an application. Cookies help the application recognize your device and store certain information about your preferences or past activities.

2. Types of Cookies We Use We use the following types of cookies in Fruutty: a. Essential Cookies These cookies are necessary for the app to function properly. They enable you to navigate the app and use its features. Without these cookies, certain services may not be available. b. Performance Cookies These cookies collect information about how you use the app, such as which pages you visit most often. This information helps us improve the app's performance and user experience. c. Functional Cookies Functional cookies allow the app to remember choices you make (such as your username or language preference) and provide enhanced, more personalized features. d. Analytics Cookies We may use analytics cookies to collect information about how users interact with the app. This data helps us analyze user behavior and improve our services.

3. How You Can Manage Cookies You can manage cookies through your device settings. Most devices allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, please note that if you disable cookies, some features of the app may not function properly. a. Mobile Device Settings For mobile devices, you can usually manage cookies in your device's settings. Please refer to your device's user manual or help section for specific instructions. b. Third-Party Cookies We may allow third parties to place cookies on your device to provide services or analytics. These third parties have their own privacy policies and practices, and we encourage you to review them.
